How to get new team members started at CPS – including self-service.

### Why onboarding matters

For new starters, a good experience when joining the CPS design team is crucial.

Having a supported and comprehensive introduction will:

- make them feel supported in their new role
- help them to understand our existing roles, structures and processes
- orientate them within the wider project they’re about to contribute to
- support cross-team collaboration
- help them to start making a positive impact as quickly as possible

### Who this guide is for

This guide can be used by anyone involved in the onboarding process, including the new starter as a resource to increase their understanding.

Check the tags in each section to understand the key people involved and responsible. For example, <strong class="govuk-tag govuk-tag--light-blue">Onboarding partner</strong>.

### Three steps to get started

The welcome process generally follows these three steps:

- Meet key people
- Set up platforms and invitations
- Understand the programme with walkthroughs
